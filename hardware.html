Supported hardware

MockTab targets Wacom USB and Bluetooth tablets from roughly 1996 through the late 2010s. The list below is split into tablets confirmed live on real hardware and tablets covered by the generic decoder pending live confirmation.

Generic-decoder support

MockTab ships a registry of roughly fifty Wacom product IDs and a generic decoder that auto-detects the report format. Most Intuos, Intuos Pro, Bamboo Pen & Touch, and Cintiq models from this era should enumerate and work, but they haven't all been confirmed on live hardware yet.

If you have a tablet not in the confirmed list and it works (or doesn't), please open an issue with the model number, USB product ID, and what you saw. Logs from the Info tab help.

Wireless

  • USB dongle (ACK-40401): works as a generic device once paired.
  • Bluetooth: supported on tablets that ship with BT (PTH-660, PTH-860). Pairing is done in macOS System Settings; MockTab picks them up automatically.

Not supported

  • Tablets from Huion, XP-Pen, Xencelabs, Gaomon, and other vendors.
  • Wacom tablets newer than ~2020 (Intuos Pro Gen 3 and successors).
  • Serial / ADB-era Wacom tablets.
  • Graphire-family and original Bamboo (CTL/CTH-4xx) pen decoders — registered for future work.
  • Touch / multitouch input — detected but disabled.
